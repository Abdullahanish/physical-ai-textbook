
# Chapter 1: Assessment Questions

Test your understanding of the chapter with these questions.

### Multiple Choice

1.  **What is the best definition of "Physical AI"?**
    a)  Any computer program that can learn.
    b)  A robot that follows a pre-programmed set of instructions.
    c)  An AI system that has a physical body and can interact with the world.
    d)  A chatbot that can understand human language.

2.  **The concept that an AI's intelligence is shaped by its physical form is known as:**
    a)  Actuation
    b)  Embodiment
    c)  Sensing
    d)  Programming

3.  **Which of the following is an example of an actuator?**
    a)  A camera on a self-driving car.
    b)  The motor in a robot's arm.
    c)  A microphone that hears commands.
    d)  The AI software that makes decisions.

4.  **What is the primary role of a sensor in a robot?**
    a)  To perform physical actions.
    -   To make decisions.
    -   To perceive the environment.
    -   To store energy.

### True or False

1.  **A purely software-based AI, like a language translation app, is an example of Physical AI.** (True / False)
2.  **In a Physical AI system, the "brain" and the "body" are completely separate and do not influence each other.** (True / False)
3.  **A humanoid robot is designed to look and move like a human.** (True / False)

### Short Answer

1.  Explain in your own words the difference between "robotics" and "AI". How do they come together to create Physical AI?
2.  Name two of the major challenges in building effective Physical AI systems.

---

### *Answers*
*   **Multiple Choice:** 1(c), 2(b), 3(b), 4(c)
*   **True or False:** 1(False), 2(False), 3(True)
