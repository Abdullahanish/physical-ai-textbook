
# Chapter 1: Learning Objectives

Welcome to the beginning of your journey into the exciting world of Physical AI and Humanoid Robotics! By the end of this chapter, you will be able to:

- **Define Physical AI:** Clearly explain what "Physical AI" means and how it's different from purely digital or software-based AI.
- **Identify Core Components:** Recognize the key parts that make up a Physical AI system, such as the body (hardware) and the brain (software).
- **Understand Embodiment:** Grasp the concept of "embodiment" and why giving AI a physical body is so important for interacting with the real world.
- **Explain the Importance of Physical AI:** Discuss why Physical AI is a critical field for the future, from helping in our homes to exploring new planets.
- **Distinguish Between AI and Robotics:** Understand the relationship and differences between Artificial Intelligence and the field of robotics.
- **Recognize Key Challenges:** Identify some of the major hurdles that engineers and researchers face when building intelligent robots.
